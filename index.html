Bio

I am a research engineer at Nebia, where we make beautiful products that help people save water and energy. Prior to that, I was a post-doctoral researcher in the Microbial Ecology and Biogeochemistry Lab with Leslie Prufert-Bebout at NASA Ames Research Center. In 2013 I earned my PhD in mechanical engineering, with a focus in Thermal and Fluid Systems, at the University of Texas at Austin. My research at UT dealt in bioreactor design for efficient algae cultivation. I earned a Bachelors of Science with Honors in mechanical engineering from Brown University. I grew up in Elmhurst, IL.
